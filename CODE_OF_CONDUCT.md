# Code of Conduct

Lambda Biolab is a space for scientists, students, and tinkerers. Participation — in repos, issues, PRs, discussions, and at the lab — is governed by this code.

## Expected

- Be respectful. Assume good faith.
- Be direct and precise. Disagree on substance, not identity.
- Help newcomers. Everyone was new once.

## Not tolerated

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Publishing others' private information.
- Sustained disruption of discussion or collaboration.

## Scope

Applies in all Lambda Biolab spaces: GitHub repos, discussions, events, and lab premises.

## Enforcement

Report violations to [contact@lambconsulting.bio](mailto:contact@lambconsulting.bio). Maintainers will respond within 7 days. Possible actions: warning, temporary ban, permanent ban.
