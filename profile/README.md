# Lambda Biolab

Basel's independent DIY molecular biology lab.

Benches, reagents, and compute for scientists, students, and tinkerers at the intersection of biology, computation, and hardware.

**[lambdabiolab.com](https://lambdabiolab.com)** · Riehenstrasse 14, 4058 Basel · BSL-1

---

### What we run

- **Wet lab** — BSL-1 bench space, cloning reagents, cell lines, eLabFTW notebooks, shared instruments.
- **Dry lab** — on-site workstation (i9 + RTX 4090), 3D printers, soldering, wiring, building.
- **Community** — hackathons, workshops, open build nights.

### What we build

**Computational biology** — drug discovery pipelines, cloud GPU orchestration ([vastai-gpu-runner](https://github.com/Lambda-Biolab/vastai-gpu-runner)), structure prediction and molecular dynamics runners ([biolab-runners](https://github.com/Lambda-Biolab/biolab-runners)), shared research and tooling ([bioml-commons](https://github.com/Lambda-Biolab/bioml-commons)).

**Genomics** — native ARM64 builds of DeepVariant for [Linux](https://github.com/Lambda-Biolab/deepvariant-linux-arm64) (Graviton, Ampere, RK3588) and [macOS Apple Silicon](https://github.com/Lambda-Biolab/deepvariant-macos-arm64-metal) with Metal GPU acceleration.

**Lab hardware** — open-source drivers and automation for PCR workstations ([bentolab](https://github.com/Lambda-Biolab/bentolab)), electronic pipettes ([dpette-usb-driver](https://github.com/Lambda-Biolab/dpette-usb-driver)), and robotic arms ([so101-biolab-automation](https://github.com/Lambda-Biolab/so101-biolab-automation)).
